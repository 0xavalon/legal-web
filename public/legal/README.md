# Legal Documents

This directory contains the legal documents (PDFs) for the application.

## Required Files

Please place the following PDF files in this directory:

- `uae-tos.pdf` - UAE Terms of Service
- `eea-tos.pdf` - EEA Terms of Service
- `uae-pp.pdf` - UAE Privacy Policy
- `eea-pp.pdf` - EEA Privacy Policy

## Source

These documents are based on the legal documents from:
<https://help.pipes.tech/en/articles/11058454-terms-of-services-and-privacy-policy>

## Instructions

1. Download the PDF documents from the source URL above
2. Rename them according to the naming convention listed above
3. Place them in this directory (`/public/legal/`)

Once added, they will be accessible at:

- `/legal/uae-tos.pdf`
- `/legal/eea-tos.pdf`
- `/legal/uae-pp.pdf`
- `/legal/eea-pp.pdf`
